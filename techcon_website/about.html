History

Our fluid dispensing systems offer improved industrial hygiene and enhanced productivity, making processes more efficient and creating value for you. Moving forward, our innovation roadmap pushes the boundaries even further, with the introduction of new intelligent solutions. These new developments reduce human error and further increase precision, while enabling a range of IoT capabilities. As always, you’re supported by a team of expert engineers who can solve your toughest manufacturing problems, whether you’re in aerospace, military, material packaging, industrial assembly, medical devices, or electronics. We’re Techcon, an OK International company, and we are committed to providing you with the highest value. As your processes and challenges evolve, Techcon will continue to deliver smarter, cleaner, more durable solutions.

Mission

Our organization embodies a commitment to fostering innovation, catalyzing investment, nurturing entrepreneurship, and influencing positive change. We pledge to cultivate an environment where creativity flourishes and bold ideas are embraced. Through strategic partnerships and targeted initiatives, we seek to drive investments into cutting-edge projects and emerging industries, fueling economic growth and technological advancement.
